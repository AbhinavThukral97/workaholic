Welcome to Workaholic!

Currently can't send notifications becuase HTTPS source required. While you take care of your work, Workaholic is an app that takes care of you. In just one day at the office, it is usual to spend about 5-6 hours staring continuously at our screens. This slowly ruins our posture, eyesight and causes headaches. In addition, being immersed in work makes us forget to take care of our hydration and other important health related daily activities. Workaholic uses the best health practices and reminds you in well thought-out intervals to do small 2 minute activities that will help improve your health as you work. You can also write notes, your to-do tasks, set goals and reminders. Just hit start working to set the timer and have a healthier, more productive day at work.
